The relationship describes how the capability is associated with the trait. A relationship of EXHIBITS indicates that the capability has a basic association to the Trait, REQUIRED indicates that the capability is dependent on the trait - for example the capability would not be available when documenting an application if that application does not exhibit the trait.

Applications may either exhibit this trait directly or be associated to a capability that is linked to this trait.
